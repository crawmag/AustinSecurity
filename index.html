Five Diamond Certified Monitoring

Austin Central Security monitoring and dispatch services are provided through United Central Control. United Central Control (UCC) – San Antonio holds “Five Diamond Certification,” a prestigous certification given by The Central Station Alarm Association (CSAA). Of roughly 2,800 central stations in the United States, UCC – San Antonio is one of less than 100 central stations to hold this certification.
